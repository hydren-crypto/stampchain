Unprunable Bitcoin Art, because Sats don’t exist.

Minting

  1. Login to Counterwallet.
  2. Create a numeric asset (not named).
  3. Convert your art into base64 encoding.
  4. Prepend STAMP: before the base64 string. Eg: STAMP:iVBORw0KGgoAAAA...
  5. Place the string into the Description field.
  6. Check the mempool to ensure that your sats/vbyte fee is adequate.
  7. Your STAMP will appear in the directory chronologically by TX Index.
